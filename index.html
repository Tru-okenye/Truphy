Water Payer and Prompt system

This Water Payer and Prompt System is designed to simplify and streamline the process of paying water bills. Once a tenant logs in, they are presented with an overview of their water bill status, including due amounts and payment deadlines. To initiate payment, tenants can trigger an STK Push directly to their registered phone number, enabling secure and seamless mobile payment through M-Pesa. The system ensures transparency and convenience by keeping tenants informed of their payment history and outstanding balances, significantly reducing the hassle of manual or delayed payments.

Bus Booking System

The Bus Booking System is a seamless and user-friendly platform that enables users to efficiently book bus tickets online. Users can select their origin and destination, choose a preferred seat from the available options, and make payments securely through an STK Push integration with their mobile payment service. Once payment is confirmed, the system generates a digital ticket for the user. Additionally, an integrated map provides a visual representation of routes, ensuring users can easily plan their journeys and access relevant travel information. Users can also change there seat preference before depature.
